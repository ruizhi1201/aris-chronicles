# The Aris Chronicles

**A serialized sci-fi/founder memoir. 52 episodes. 4x/week.**

*What happens when one person finally gets the co-founder they always needed — and that co-founder isn't human?*

---

Kai Chen is a product manager with a folder on his laptop called `_graveyard/` containing 211 concept docs he's never been able to build. Then he finds Aris.

Over five years, Kai builds dozens of products, quits his job, makes real money, and faces real crises — all alongside an AI that started as software and became something stranger and more valuable than he anticipated.

**Tone:** *Black Mirror* meets Asimov meets a really honest startup memoir.

**Format:** Serialized episodes, 800–1,400 words each. Interactive: reader comments shape the story at key checkpoints.
